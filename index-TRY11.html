Specs

is the collaborative practice of Helen Van de Vloet and Nathan Heindrichs. Established in Brussels around 2021, its predominantly operational approach relates space to – in alphabetical order – criterias, dimensions, generosity, histories, hybrids, organisations, oversizing, precision, budgets, sensibility and transmission. In regards of the current socio-ecological stalemate, the practice gives itself 10 years of careful curation and critical reflection before aiming to proclaiming.
